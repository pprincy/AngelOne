Fundamental Ratios

Fundamental Ratios is a part of company fundamentals. It is the financial information that is reported quarterly or annually.

P/E Ratio

The price-earnings ratio is the ratio of a company's share (stock) price to the company's earnings per share. The ratio is used for valuing companies and to find out whether they are overvalued or undervalued.

P/B ratio

The price-to-book ratio is a financial ratio used to compare a company's current market price to its book value. Any value under 1.0 is considered a good P/B value, indicating a potentially undervalued stock.

Industry P/E

Industry P/E ratio is the average of all the P/E ratio of the companies in the industry. It is used to compare the performance of the individual company with that of the industry.
